# Email Mode

The Email mode in our system allows you to interact with and manage email communications efficiently.

## Overview

Email mode provides functionality for handling email-based interactions and tasks.

## Features

- Email composition and sending
- Email inbox management
- Email template support
- Attachment handling
- Email signature management

## Configuration

To configure email mode, you'll need to:

1. Set up your email credentials

3. Define any custom email templates
