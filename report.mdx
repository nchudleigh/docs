## Reporting Process

The fastest way to get support is via email at support@superwhisper.com. 
Follow the steps below and include the screenshots & information requested to resolve your issue quickly.


## Enable Error Reporting

<Steps>
  1. Open the Configuration page in SuperWhisper settings
  2. Toggle the error logging switch to enable it
</Steps>

  <img src="/images/report-turn-on-error-logging.png" alt="Turn on error logging" />
</Frame>
